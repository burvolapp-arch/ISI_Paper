\begin{abstract}
\noindent
\textit{Analytical brief based on the ISI Paper
Series~(Papers~1--2).  Data basis: EU-27, Methodology~v1.0,
Vintage~2024.  The \ISI is a six-dimensional composite indicator
of external supplier concentration.  This document summarises the
principal empirical results and provides a structural
interpretation.  The contribution is a systematic quantification
of structural heterogeneity within the EU-27.}
\end{abstract}
